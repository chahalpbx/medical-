Product Description

Paracetamol is a widely used over-the-counter pain reliever and fever reducer. It's typically used to treat mild to moderate pain and fever.

This medication works by blocking the production of certain natural substances that cause pain and fever. It does not affect the clotting of blood and does not irritate the stomach as much as other pain relievers.

Key Benefits:

  • Relieves mild to moderate pain
  • Reduces fever
  • Gentle on the stomach
  • Can be taken with or without food

Usage & Dosage

Take this medication by mouth as directed. Follow all directions on the product package. If you are uncertain about any of the information, consult your doctor or pharmacist.

If you are taking this medication for self-treatment, it is important to read the package instructions carefully before you start using this product to know the correct dose.

Standard Dosage:

  • Adults and children 12 years and older: 1-2 tablets every 4-6 hours as needed
  • Do not take more than 8 tablets in 24 hours
  • Do not use for more than 10 days unless directed by a doctor

Warning: Taking more than the recommended dose can cause serious harm to your liver. Do not use with other medicines containing paracetamol.
